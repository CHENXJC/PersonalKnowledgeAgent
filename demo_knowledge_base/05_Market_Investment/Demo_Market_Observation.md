# Demo Market Observation

## Topic

Educational observation about small business software adoption.

## Observation

Small teams often adopt workflow software when three conditions are visible:

- A repeated manual task consumes measurable time.
- The current process creates missed follow-ups or reporting delays.
- The first automation step can be piloted without changing the whole business.

## Possible Research Questions

- Which manual tasks are easiest to standardize first?
- How can a dashboard show workflow pain without exposing private records?
- What proof is needed before a team trusts a new automation process?
