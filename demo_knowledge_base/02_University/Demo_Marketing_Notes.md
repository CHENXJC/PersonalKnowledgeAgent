# Demo Marketing Notes

## Topic

Segmentation, targeting, and positioning for a fictional small business.

## Summary

The demo business serves two customer groups:

- Busy students who want a faster way to organize assignments.
- Small teams that want lightweight reporting templates.

## Framework

- Segmentation: group users by workflow pain and usage context.
- Targeting: prioritize the group with the clearest repeated problem.
- Positioning: communicate one simple outcome instead of listing every feature.
