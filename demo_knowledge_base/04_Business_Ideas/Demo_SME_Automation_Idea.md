# Demo SME Automation Idea

## Idea

A local-first automation audit for a fictional small tutoring studio.

## Problem

The team receives inquiries from several channels, copies details into a
spreadsheet, and manually prepares a weekly status update.

## Lightweight MVP

1. Create a structured intake table.
2. Define lead status labels.
3. Add a weekly summary template.
4. Track response time before and after the pilot.

## Risk Controls

- Do not automate private customer communication without review.
- Do not store real contact details in public examples.
- Use fictional records for screenshots and demos.

## Public-Safe Note

This business idea is fictional and does not contain real client data.
