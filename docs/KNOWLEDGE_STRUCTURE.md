# Knowledge Structure

PersonalKnowledgeAgent uses a local Markdown-first folder structure. PKB-001 only creates the structure and sample templates. Real private notes should be imported carefully in later stages.

## 01_AI_Projects

Store notes for AI Agent, AI Skill, Streamlit, Vibecoding, GitHub portfolio, testing, documentation, and showcase projects.

Recommended content:

- Project status notes.
- Checkpoint history.
- Feature summaries.
- Testing notes.
- Public showcase preparation notes.

## 02_University

Store university learning material and study notes.

Recommended content:

- Marketing course notes.
- Assignment planning notes.
- Case study summaries.
- Exam revision outlines.

Do not store student ID documents, official visa files, passports, bank statements, or other highly sensitive documents.

## 03_Career

Store career planning and job-search material.

Recommended content:

- Resume idea notes.
- Portfolio positioning notes.
- Interview preparation notes.
- Internship and graduate role research.

Do not store private identity documents or employer portal credentials.

## 04_Business_Ideas

Store business idea notes and AI automation service concepts.

Recommended content:

- Target user pain points.
- Information gap and cognition gap notes.
- MVP ideas.
- Monetization paths.
- Portfolio value.
- Risk notes.

## 05_Market_Investment

Store market observation and investment research notes for learning and portfolio analysis only.

Recommended content:

- Market trend observations.
- Industry research.
- Watchlist notes.
- Public data interpretation notes.

This category is for research, education, and portfolio analysis only. It is not financial advice and must not include real trading automation.

## 06_Personal_Archive

Store low-risk personal archive notes that are useful for long-term learning.

Recommended content:

- Personal learning reflections.
- Weekly review notes.
- Idea parking lot notes.

Do not store credentials, identity documents, banking files, medical files, or other sensitive personal records.
